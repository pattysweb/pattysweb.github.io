In this project, our assignment asked us to hand code a website from scratch. We had to construct a website that involved something in our life today or something that was going on with the world today. I decided to base my website on the EDM culture since it recently changed my life in a positive way. It still has an impact and the purpose behind it was to show others how it can do the same for you. I would say this website is mostly for those who are genuinely interested in the music and want to know what to expect when it comes to their first rave.

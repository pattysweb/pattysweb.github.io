DIMENSION 3: THESE ACTIVITIES ADD TO THE EXPERIENCE

Dancing is definitely one of the activities, but ravers have found a way to make the experience a bit more enhanced and that's with flow art. It's kind of like dancing, but with hands. Not everyone is into this, but they either like to do a show or enjoy it. I love to work with these lights, especially the LED whip. I gloved for a bit, but the whip has my heart. (No not that kind of whip) We flow artists, love to find creative ways to make the show a part of your unforgettable experience.


You've got your Glovers : The ones who dance with their hands




My personal favorite : LED Whip Artists

Innovative way of showing lights with fibers




Hoop Artists : A hoola-hoop but with interchangeable LED lights that make hoola-hooping a lot more interesting


The Space Saber : This one is the newest type of flow art. Has an interesting concept to it and literally looks like you're levitating a wand


There's a lot more types of flow arts, but from my experience these are the only types I've seen so far and actually have tried

MEME MASHUP

Patricia Chavez

Memes are culturally significant and extremely popular in our society today. It's a way for people to express their humor, even if it's a bit dark. I decided to use famous characters such as Spongebob and Patrick. They have a special place in my heart since it is one of my favorite childhood TV shows and I'm sure it's the same for many. I'm not going to lie, I'm not into memes as many people in my generation are... so my skills at making them aren't so great. It was fun to actually get to make one though since I know I would never actually do it on my free time.

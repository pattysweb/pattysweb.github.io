GLITCH ART

Patricia Chavez

This was an interesting project and probably one of my favorites. I was so happy when it came to the results because it turned out better than expected. It's also an amazing feeling when you have no idea what you're doing and you finally figure it out and turns out to be awesome. The first image show is done with text editor. This one was a bit difficult at first, but once I got the hang of it, I actually had fun experimenting with this one. The rest of the buddha images were done with an audio application. I never thought you could glitch an image with an audio player until now.

3D PRINT

Patricia Chavez

After creating our Minecraft sculpture, we were asked to print it out in a 3D form. This excited me in so many ways, because I got the opportunity to bring my art to life with a 3D machine. It was a complex process, especially for someone who's never done it before, but when the print was finished, I was very satisfied with the results.

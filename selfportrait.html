SELF PORTRAIT

Patricia Chavez

This project was probably one of the hardest and most tedious we worked on this semester. A lot of trial and error, took me hours to finally get what I wanted, but I finally completed this masterpiece. We were asked to portray our personality, so I decided to add an angel halo. I find myself to be a genuineperson, so the halo was a nice touch.
